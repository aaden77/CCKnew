About CCK

The Compassion Corporation of Kingston (CCK) was established many years ago with the mission to take an active interest in the civic, cultural, social, and moral welfare of the community. We strive to nurture compassion, drive positive change, and empower our citizens to create a thriving, inclusive and vibrant community.

Membership Information

Join The Compassion Corporation of Kingston (CCK) and become part of a community dedicated to compassion, civic engagement, and positive change.

Types of Membership

  • Student Membership – For individuals currently enrolled in an academic institution.
  • Associate Membership – For supporters who wish to contribute and participate without full voting rights.
  • Full Membership – For active members with full participation and voting privileges.
  • Life-Time Membership – For long-term supporters who commit to a permanent membership.

How to Apply

To apply for membership, please complete our online application form. Once submitted, our team will review your application and contact you with next steps.
